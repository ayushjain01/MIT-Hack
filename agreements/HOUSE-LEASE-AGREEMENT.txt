HOUSE LEASE AGREEMENT

This House Lease Agreement (the "Agreement") is entered into between Bharath, hereinafter referred to as the "Landlord," and Raghu, hereinafter referred to as the "Tenant," collectively referred to as the "Parties." This Agreement is effective as of 10th may 2023.

1. Property Details

1.1 Landlord hereby leases to the Tenant and Tenant hereby leases from the Landlord, the residential property located at [Property Address] (the "Property"), including all fixtures, furnishings, and appliances specified in Schedule A attached hereto.
 
2. Lease Term

2.1 The initial term of this lease shall be 5 years commencing on 10th may 2023 and ending on 9th may 2028. The Tenant shall vacate the Property at the end of the lease term unless a renewal or extension is agreed upon in writing by both Parties.

3. Rent and Payment Terms

3.1 The Tenant shall pay a monthly rent of 20 thousand on or before the 10th of each month. The first month's rent shall be due on 9th before the Tenant's occupancy of the Property.

3.2 Rent payments shall be made in the form of [Payment Method] to the Landlord or as directed by the Landlord in writing.

3.3 In the event of late payment, the Tenant shall be responsible for the payment of late fees as specified in Schedule B attached hereto.

4. Security Deposit

4.1 The Tenant shall provide a security deposit of 2 Lakh upon signing this Agreement. The security deposit shall be held by the Landlord as security for the performance of Tenant's obligations under this Agreement.

4.2 The security deposit shall be refundable within 30 days of the termination of this Agreement, subject to deductions for any unpaid rent, damages beyond normal wear and tear, or any other expenses incurred by the Landlord due to the Tenant's breach of this Agreement.

5. Maintenance and Repairs

5.1 The Tenant agrees to maintain the Property in good condition and promptly report any damages or necessary repairs to the Landlord.

5.2 Minor repairs and maintenance caused by normal wear and tear shall be the responsibility of the Tenant. Major repairs and structural maintenance shall be the responsibility of the Landlord.

6. Utilities and Other Expenses

6.1 The Tenant shall be responsible for all utilities, including but not limited to electricity, gas, water, sewerage, internet, cable TV, and telephone services, unless otherwise specified in Schedule C attached hereto.

7. Termination

7.1 Either Party may terminate this Agreement in accordance with the following terms:

7.1.1 The Tenant may terminate this Agreement by providing written notice of 30 days to the Landlord.

7.1.2 The Landlord may terminate this Agreement for cause, including but not limited to non-payment of rent, violation of lease terms, or breach of any applicable laws or regulations.

7.2 Upon termination, the Tenant shall vacate the Property and return the keys to the Landlord, leaving the Property in a clean and undamaged condition, subject to normal wear and tear.

8. Governing Law and Jurisdiction

8.1 This Agreement shall be governed by and construed in accordance with the laws of [Jurisdiction]. Any disputes arising out of or in connection with this Agreement shall be resolved exclusively by the courts of mysore.

9. Entire Agreement

9.1 This Agreement constitutes the entire agreement between the Parties and supersedes all prior discussions, negotiations, and agreements, whether oral or written, relating to the subject matter hereof.
